About Us

"Web based Plant-Nature Identification" is one of the best platform that provides the indigenous knowledge about plant intelligence and plantation of different types of plants. Its basic purpose is to help peoples in protecting plants, protecting unhealthy plantation, making peoples familiar about different measures taken in plantation for different types of plants and co-creation with nature which benefits the wellbeing of both the peoples and planet.

Our Motive..

Our mission is to inspire people about having healthy plantation and exploring the intelligence behind this wonderful nature which we call our home. we also want to create a global community through this platform that nature wisdom to be accessible to people from all the corners of the world.
