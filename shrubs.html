Shrubs,

Know everything about it..

A shrub is defined as a woody perennial plant, having several
major stems arising from at or near the base.
Shrubs are distinguished from trees by their multiple
stems and shorter height, usually under 6 m tall.
Shrubs generally have dense foliage, that is, many small,
leafy branches growing close together.
The leaves can be either deciduous or evergreen.
There are approximately 30,000 species of
shrubs worldwide.Most species are flowering plants.
