Climber,

Know everything about it..

Climbers are much more advanced than creepers.
Climbers have a very thin, long and weak stem
which cannot stand upright, but they can
use external support to grow vertically and
carry their weight. These types of plants
use special structures called tendrils to climb.
Few climbers plants names include pea plant, grapevine,
money plant, jasmine, runner beans, green peas, etc.
